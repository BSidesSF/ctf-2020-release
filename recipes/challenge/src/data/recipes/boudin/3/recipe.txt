California Bruschetta
Ingredients

2 Boudin Sourdough Longs
5 Tbsp. Butter (softened)
8 oz. Sonoma Dry Jack Cheese (grated)
5 Cloves of Garlic (finely chopped)
Tomato Basil Topper
1 Tbsp. Parsley (chopped)



Recipe

Slice Sourdough into approximately half inch slices.
Mix chopped garlic with softened butter.
Thinly spread bread slices with garlic butter.
Broil slices until lightly toasted; remove from oven.
In a bowl, mix cheese and parsley.
Top Sourdough toast with cheese mixture.
Bake in preheated 375Â° F oven for 5-10 minutes, or until cheese has melted.
Top with Tomato Basil Topper.
Serve.
